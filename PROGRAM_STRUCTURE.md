## Detailed Program Descriptions

### 1. Training Programs

*   **Insights** (`/programs/training/insights`)
    *   *Focus:* Practical market and self-awareness inputs.
    *   *Goal:* Helps business owners see their next major decision with absolute clarity.
*   **Organization Training** (`/programs/training/organization-training`)
    *   *Focus:* Team communication, alignment, ownership, and execution.
    *   *Goal:* Streamlining corporate dynamics and building self-running organizational systems.
*   **Breakthrough** (`/programs/training/breakthrough`)
    *   *Focus:* Overcoming psychological and strategy roadblocks.
    *   *Goal:* Moving past stagnated growth points with confidence and NLP reframing.
*   **Sarathi** (`/programs/training/sarathi`)
    *   *Focus:* High-proximity elite personal mentorship.
    *   *Goal:* Providing direct, 1-on-1 crisis advisory, board support, and family business alignment.
*   **Beyond the Classroom** (`/programs/training/beyond-the-classroom`)
    *   *Focus:* Outdoor experiential learning and real-world situations.
    *   *Goal:* Connecting business theories with visceral, active scenarios to cement leadership patterns.
*   **YOUNG ADULTS** (`/programs/training/young-adults`)
    *   *Focus:* Purpose alignment, career focus, and emotional intelligence.
    *   *Goal:* Preparing the next generation of leaders (aged 16-25) to navigate responsibilities, scaling, and personal values.

### 2. 1 Day Training Programs

*   **Goal Setting** (`/programs/1-day/goal-setting`)
    *   *Focus:* Frameworks for personal/team clarity and action lists.
    *   *Goal:* Turning big ideas into immediate daily execution schedules.
*   **Business Communication** (`/programs/1-day/business-communication`)
    *   *Focus:* Team communication, active listening, delegation, and reporting templates.
    *   *Goal:* Eradicating operational errors and streamlining department workflows.
*   **Leadership and Negotiation** (`/programs/1-day/leadership-and-negotiation`)
    *   *Focus:* Negotiation psychology, boardroom presence, and decision matrices.
    *   *Goal:* Equipping leaders to handle complex deals, steer teams, and manage key client disputes.

### 3. Relationship Tourism

*   **Relationship Tourism** (`/programs/relationship-tourism`)
    *   *Focus:* Experiential bonding retreats.
    *   *Goal:* Building deep trust, resolving generational/founder differences, and aligning visions in custom-curated, distraction-free travel environments.
